◆ LevelPhase

enum Underconnected.LevelPhase
strong

Contains phases for game levels.

Enumerator
Init 

The initial state.

Preparing 

The preparing state which shows a timer running down.

Playing 

The level is being played.

Players can move around and deliver recipes.

Finished 

The level is finished.

Players can no longer move and see a 'finished' screen.

◆ RecipeMatchState

enum Underconnected.RecipeMatchState
strong

Tells how much of a recipe has been matched.

Enumerator
NoMatch 

The recipe has not been matched at all (there are foreign elements which are not allowed).

PartialMatch 

The recipe has been partially matched (there are a few missing elements).

FullMatch 

The recipe has been fully matched and can produce its output.
